# Organizing Your Tunes

FolkTunesApp provides several ways to organize your tune library: genres, tags, custom fields, and instruments.

## Genres

Genres help you categorize tunes by musical tradition or style (e.g., Irish, Scottish, English, American).

### Managing Genres

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open Settings</strong>
    <p>Tap the gear icon in the bottom navigation bar.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Navigate to Genre Settings</strong>
    <p>In the "Tune Organization" section, tap "Manage Genres".</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Genre settings screen</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Add a genre</strong>
    <p>Tap the "+" button to add a new genre. Enter the genre name and tap "Save".</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">4</span>
    <strong>Edit or delete genres</strong>
    <p>Swipe left on a genre to edit or delete it.</p>
</div>

### Assigning Genres to Tunes

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open a tune</strong>
    <p>From the Tunes tab, tap on a tune to view its details.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Tap Edit</strong>
    <p>Tap the "Edit" button to modify the tune.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Select a genre</strong>
    <p>Use the genre picker to assign a genre to the tune.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Genre picker in tune edit screen</div>
</div>

## Tags

Tags provide flexible organization. A tune can have multiple tags, making it easy to find tunes by mood, difficulty, occasion, or any other category.

### Adding Tags to Tunes

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open a tune</strong>
    <p>Navigate to the tune's detail view.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Tap Edit</strong>
    <p>Tap the "Edit" button.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Add tags</strong>
    <p>In the tags field, type a tag name and tap "Add". You can add multiple tags to a single tune.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Tag field in tune edit screen</div>
</div>

### Using Tags for Search

Tags can be used in advanced search to find tunes that match specific criteria.

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Advanced search with tag filter</div>
</div>

## Custom Fields

Custom fields let you track additional information about your tunes that isn't covered by the default fields.

### Creating Custom Fields

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open Settings</strong>
    <p>Go to Settings > Tune Organization.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Open Field Manager</strong>
    <p>Tap "Manage Fields".</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Field manager screen</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Add a custom field</strong>
    <p>Tap the "+" button to create a new field. Enter the field name and type (text, number, date, etc.).</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: New custom field form</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">4</span>
    <strong>Save the field</strong>
    <p>Tap "Save" to add the custom field. It will now appear in tune edit screens.</p>
</div>

### Using Custom Fields

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Edit a tune</strong>
    <p>Open a tune and tap "Edit".</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Fill in custom fields</strong>
    <p>Scroll to the custom fields section and fill in the values.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Custom fields in tune edit screen</div>
</div>

### Field Visibility

You can control which fields are visible in the tune detail view.

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open Field Manager</strong>
    <p>Go to Settings > Tune Organization > Manage Fields.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Toggle field visibility</strong>
    <p>Use the visibility toggles to show or hide fields in the tune detail view.</p>
</div>

## Instruments

If you play multiple instruments, you can track which instrument you used for each practice session.

### Enabling Instrument Tracking

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open Settings</strong>
    <p>Go to Settings > Practice Settings.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Enable instrument tracking</strong>
    <p>Toggle "Track Instruments" to enable.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Instrument tracking toggle in settings</div>
</div>

### Managing Instruments

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open Instrument Settings</strong>
    <p>With instrument tracking enabled, tap "Manage Instruments" in Settings.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Instrument management screen</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Add an instrument</strong>
    <p>Tap the "+" button to add a new instrument. Enter the instrument name and tap "Save".</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Edit or delete instruments</strong>
    <p>Swipe left on an instrument to edit or delete it.</p>
</div>

### Selecting an Instrument During Practice

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Start a practice session</strong>
    <p>When you start a practice session with instrument tracking enabled, you'll be prompted to select an instrument.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Instrument selection at start of practice session</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Choose an instrument</strong>
    <p>Select from your list of instruments, or enter a custom instrument name.</p>
</div>
