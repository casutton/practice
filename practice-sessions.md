# Practice Sessions

Practice sessions are the core of FolkTunesApp. Each session lets you practice tunes, track your progress, and rate your performance.

## Starting a Practice Session

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Navigate to Practice tab</strong>
    <p>Tap the play button icon in the bottom navigation bar.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Practice tab showing "Start Practice Session" button</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Tap "Start Practice Session"</strong>
    <p>This begins a new practice session and starts the session timer.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Select an instrument (optional)</strong>
    <p>If instrument tracking is enabled in Settings, you'll be prompted to select which instrument you're practicing with.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Instrument selection screen</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>

    <strong>Search for a specific tune</strong>
    <p>Use the search bar to find a tune by title, composer, or tags.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Tune picker with search functionality</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>

</div>

## Practice Tools

The session timer automatically tracks how long you've been practicing. It starts when you begin a session and continues until you end the session.

### Session Notes

You can add notes during or after a practice session.

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Add session notes</strong>
    <p>During a session, tap the notes field to add general notes about your practice.</p>

<div class="flow-step">

## Rating Your Practice

After practicing a tune, you'll be asked to rate your performance.

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Finish practicing a tune</strong>
    <p>When you're done with a tune, you'll see the practice rating screen.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Practice rating screen with tune title and rating options</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Select a rating</strong>
    <p>Choose one of three options:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Difficult</strong> - You struggled with this tune or made mistakes</li>
        <li><strong>Good</strong> - You played it reasonably well with minor issues</li>
        <li><strong>Easy</strong> - You played it confidently and accurately</li>
    </ul>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Add notes (optional)</strong>
    <p>You can add notes about what you worked on, what was challenging, or what to focus on next time.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Notes field in practice rating screen</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">4</span>
    <strong>Save the rating</strong>
    <p>Tap "Save" to record your rating. The tune will be scheduled for review based on your rating.</p>
</div>

<div class="info">
    <strong>How ratings affect scheduling:</strong>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Difficult</strong> → Tune moves to "Next Session" bucket (appears again soon)</li>
        <li><strong>Good</strong> → Tune moves up one bucket (longer interval)</li>
        <li><strong>Easy</strong> → Tune moves up one bucket (even longer interval)</li>
    </ul>
</div>

## Canceling a Session

If you need to cancel a session without saving:

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Tap "Cancel Session"</strong>
    <p>This option is available during an active session.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Confirm cancellation</strong>
    <p>You'll be asked to confirm. Canceling will discard the session and any unsaved progress.</p>
</div>

<div class="warning">
    <strong>Note:</strong> Canceling a session will not save any practice time, tune ratings, or notes. Make sure you want to discard this session before confirming.
</div>
