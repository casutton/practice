### Tips for Screenshots:
- Use iPhone screenshots (preferably iPhone 15 Pro or similar modern device)
- Ensure good lighting and clear text
- Show the relevant UI elements clearly
- For dialogs/sheets, show them in their active state
- For forms, show them with some example data filled in
- For lists, show 2-3 items to give context
- Capture at the right moment (e.g., with search results visible, with goals showing progress)
