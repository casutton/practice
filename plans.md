# Practice Plans

Practice plans help you organize your practice by grouping specific tunes and exercises together. Plans are useful for learning sets of related tunes or preparing for performances.

## Creating a Practice Plan

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Navigate to Plans</strong>
    <p>Go to the Improve tab, then select the Plans section.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Plans view showing list of practice plans</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Tap the add button</strong>
    <p>Tap the "+" button to create a new plan.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Enter plan details</strong>
    <p>Fill in the plan form:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Title</strong> - Name of the plan (e.g., "Irish Session Set")</li>
        <li><strong>Description</strong> - What the plan is for (optional)</li>
    </ul>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: New plan form</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">4</span>
    <strong>Add tunes to the plan</strong>
    <p>Select tunes from your library to include in the plan.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Adding tunes to a practice plan</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">5</span>
    <strong>Save the plan</strong>
    <p>Tap "Save" to create the plan.</p>
</div>

## Using a Practice Plan

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Start a practice session</strong>
    <p>Go to the Practice tab and start a practice session.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Select a plan</strong>
    <p>During a practice session, you can choose to practice from a specific plan.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Plan selection during practice session</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Practice tunes from the plan</strong>
    <p>The app will suggest tunes from your selected plan.</p>
</div>

## Editing Plans

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">1</span>
    <strong>Open a plan</strong>
    <p>From the Plans view, tap on a plan to view its details.</p>
</div>

<div class="screenshot placeholder">
    <div class="caption">Screenshot: Plan detail view showing included tunes</div>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">2</span>
    <strong>Tap Edit</strong>
    <p>Tap the "Edit" button to modify the plan.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">3</span>
    <strong>Make changes</strong>
    <p>Update the plan title, description, or add/remove tunes.</p>
</div>

<div class="flow-step">
    <span class="step-number">4</span>
    <strong>Save changes</strong>
    <p>Tap "Save" to update the plan.</p>
</div>
